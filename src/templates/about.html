About Us

Our Goal

Our goal is to create a platform that enables staff to interact and communicate more through their favourite hobbies. We aim to encourage people to join groups of people taking part in new hobbies to break the ice, try something new, and also find likeminded individuals around the office that enjoy similar hobbies.

What We Do

Our website creates a safe space where people can try new hobbies, plan meetups and make new friends with likeminded people. We wanted to find a way for people to network around the office and this website enables people to find those with the same hobbies within the work environment with people they might not interact with on a regular basis.
